This is a census of the data holdings of nine key datasets by U.S. states, conducted by U.S. Open Data. This is a work in progress. Thirty-nine states have been surveyed so far, working in descending order of population, covering over 90% of the U.S. population. Bulk census data may be obtained via Google Sheets.
